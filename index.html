The gap ServiceIQ was built to close

Three pilot workflows

Measurable. Revenue-linked.
Easy to start.

The 6–12 month founding pilot focuses on one store and three workflows. Nothing else is on the table until the numbers justify expansion.

01
Declined-service recovery
When a customer declines work, ServiceIQ drafts a benefit-forward follow-up message. Your advisor reviews and approves before it sends. Multi-step sequence — 2 hours, Day 3, Day 7, Day 30. Safety-critical items are flagged with elevated urgency.
AI drafts · Advisor approves
02
New-buyer first-service outreach
When a vehicle is registered, ServiceIQ tracks the OEM maintenance schedule from the in-service date. At the right mileage, a benefit-forward message goes out automatically — before the customer's first service interval expires.
OEM schedule driven
03
Appointment-booking recommendations
AI surfaces the right services at booking using the customer's mileage and OEM schedule. Benefit-forward language is approved once at setup and runs automatically at every booking and check-in — no advisor action needed.
Approved once · Runs always
